Vision and Value

Our Vision

To be number 1 in the food import and export market in providing quality and best industry standards

Our Mission

To cover up remote country sides and regions of the globe uncovered. To provide the planet earth easy accessible and economical food stuffs. In 2030 we will be covering whole EU regions and to strengthen relationship between Nigeria and International Community with aim to bring out the best from Nigeria.
JERRILODS NIGERIA LIMITED has a vision to reach to the Mars for the imports and exports for generation next.

Our Value

Creative & Innovative

We affirm to create unique products. This, we would attain through a sustained process of innovation and indigenization. We will be a creative and thinking company that shall explore newer markets and better product with sustained efforts of product development and research.

Customer delight is our purpose

Customer delight is our core philosophy. Customer drive our passion for innovative solution and help us deliver result that have a global bearing. In building lasting relation with customers, we will forge bonds for future successes.

Best in-class Quality

We are dedicated to the highest level of quality in everything we do. Be it technology, people, systems or processes, we keep raising the bar for ourselves ensuring the best at all times.

Ownership & Team Work

We are accountable for consistent and sustainable results by focusing on present and future opportunities, eliminating obstacles internal & external. By recruiting ethical and talented people, we inspire out team to be accountable and pursue excellence. As a team, we are led by a common goal to deliver the best at all times.

About Us

JERRILODS Foods IMPEX is a division of Jerrilods Nigeria Limited is in the business of import and export of Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), Nigeria finished goods, Nigeria local foods stuff and Agricultural commodity crops and produce to meet individual and large bulk buyers. Being in the business of Exports and Imports demands a global presence. The organization since its inception has worked hard to spread wing globally.
